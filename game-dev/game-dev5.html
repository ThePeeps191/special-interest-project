The Community

The game development community is one of the most populated communities. Many indie game developers work together and talk on platforms like GitHub, Itch.io, and Stack Overflow. Game dev can be either independent or be in a group of many. Working with others can greatly improve your skills by sharing ideas and helping each other. Working with others for a game can also make the entire process faster and add many new ideas.

In other words, game dev gives you an immense community of people who share your interest and can work with you.
