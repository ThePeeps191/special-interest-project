My Games

I've published two games in my "career" with game dev. The first one is a 2D infinite platformer game. You're in a 2D world with a moving platform that slowly moves faster. You can play it infinitely until you fall off a platform and land in the lava. Your objective is to collect as many points as possible before you die. I made this game for a game jam that I got second and got a $10 Amazon gift card.

My second game is one I'm currently working on. It's a 3D fps where you are in a 3D map and shoot people in a first-person perspective. The objective is to kill all the drones and turrets with different guns. You win if you successfully kill all the enemies without dying. I am working on this for fun and am planning to publish it.
