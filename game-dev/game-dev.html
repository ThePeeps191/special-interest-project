What is Game Development?

Game development shortend to game dev is pretty self explanatory. Its the process of making games for people to play and enjoy. When you start off coding, you can move in many different directions. Game dev is one of those many directions that you can pursue, including web dev, AI or machine learning.

There are many reasons why you should start and pursue game dev. Many people don't are bored and want to start something knew, something that they can countinue for the rest of their lives. Game dev is a enourmous counmunity of people dedicated to their work and ,aking games is a way of connecting to others, meeting many people that share you interest and adding to the community. You feel proud when you take time to make a game, see many people enjoying it, and feel that you've actually accomplished something in your life. And if you add a bit of ads or something you can buy, you might even get a bit of money out of it.
